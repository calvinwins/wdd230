Target Audience

The Greater Bentonville Commerce target audience is local business owners. We look for both established and new start ups. We do not discremenate because of sex or age. We aim to build a community that can support eachother and accomplish more than a singular business.

Matt is 32 year old and is living in Bentonville. He is a father of 3 and he has started up a new fast food franchise. He is looking to make contacts and be able to become a reliable business for the community.

Company: Lexis Maximus LLC

Position: Manager

 Problem Statement: HLeadGen campaign is at 38% conversion rate(+-1.5%). As call volume increases overall conversion rate subsequently decreases. What improvements can be made to increase conversion rate and handle higher call volume?

Solution: Perform structured experiments with 3 different call scripts and utilize statistical principles (P-value, standard deviation) to determine optimizations in call script dialogue. The first call script will be the control and will use the current call script; labeled “Control”. The Second call script will be optimized for efficiency and cut down Inbound call duration; labeled “Script 1”. Lastly the third call script will focus on customer satisfaction with elevated language. It will be Focused on quality of service and labeled “Script 2”.

Result:It was discovered that a call script optimized to efficiency (script 1) lead to a decrease in calls entering queue and an overall decrease in call duration. This in turn led to an increase in total conversion rate by 8.37%.

DISCOVER

Fun at the Amazeum

One of Bentonville's top attractions and a fun family experience, the Amazeum. The Amazeum promotes an atmosphere of fun creative learning for all ages.

Biking trails

The mountain biking capitol, Bentonville offers the variety any seasoned moutain biker could desire. From high intensity to comfortable rides, there is no shortage of options.

City hall

For local meetings and to contact government officials, City hall is open for all guests. The hours of operations are Monday-Saturday 8am-5pm.

Football

The football culture is thriving in northwest Arkansas. Highschool football has a city wide cult following. You can catch a game every other Saturday starting on August 3.

The Mayor

Mayor Mike is an open and carismatic mayor. He is a local resident of 40 years. You can Contact him any time during the week at his local office on J street.

Citizen's Park

A new and exciting park for ages 5-12. Citizen's park provides more advanced equipment that promotes physicall activity and creativity.

Town Square

The Bentonville Town Square is a small gem in Arkansas. It takes you back to a simpler time. Small restuarants and an affordable ice cream shop can be enjoyed year round and on the last Friday of every month there is a festival.

Walmart Facilities

New Walmart facilities are almost complete. The facilities will include a new fitness center and a open campus for collegues to converse and experience the beautiful nature of Northwest Arkansas.

Community Center

The local Community center provides many convient and rare ammenities that will give anyone something to do year round. The indoor water park is great for all ages and is heated during those cold winters every year. Open Monday-Saturday 8am-7pm
